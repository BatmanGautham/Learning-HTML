Whats a Car?

  1. Moving Box:

    A car is like a special box with wheels that takes you places.

  2. Engine Heart:

    Cars have a magic heart called an engine that makes them go.

  3. Turn and Go:

    You use a steering wheel to tell the car where to go—left or right!

  4. Comfy Ride:

    Inside, there are comfy seats, like a moving chair.

  5. Window Views:

    Windows let you peek outside while the car moves.

  6. Treasure Trunk:

    The back is a trunk where you keep special things.

  7. Gasoline Energy:

    Cars need a special juice called gasoline to run.

  8. Traffic Rules:

    Cars follow rules like stopping at red lights and going on green lights.

  9. Adventure Machine:

    Your car is like a magic machine that can take you on fun adventures.

  10. Anywhere, Anytime:

    Cars can go to the park, school, house—anywhere you want!
