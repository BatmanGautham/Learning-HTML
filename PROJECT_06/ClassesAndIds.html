1.Pizza

5. Cool and Serve:

2.Gulab Jamun

Ingredients:

For Gulab Jamun:
   - 1 cup milk powder
   - 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
   - 1/4 cup ghee
   - 2-3 tablespoons milk
   - 1/4 teaspoon baking soda

For Sugar Syrup:
   - 1 cup sugar
   - 1/2 cup water
   - 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
   - A few saffron strands (optional)
   - 1 teaspoon rose water (optional)

Instructions:

1. Prepare Dough:
   - Mix milk powder, flour, ghee, baking soda, and enough milk to form a soft dough.
2. Shape Balls:
   - Roll the dough into smooth balls, ensuring no cracks.
3. Fry:
   - Fry balls in hot ghee or oil until golden brown.
4. Sugar Syrup:
   - Boil sugar and water to make a syrup. Add cardamom, saffron, and rose water.
5. Soak:
   - Drop fried balls into warm syrup; let them soak for 1-2 hours.
6. Serve:
   - Gulab Jamuns are ready! Garnish with nuts if desired.

Enjoy your homemade Gulab Jamuns!
